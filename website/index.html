Welcome to the website of the MIT Assistive Technology Club. We are a MIT student group (founded in 2011) that serves as the starting point for engaging with MIT students, faculty, and staff about assistive technology for people with disabilities.

assistivetechnology@mit.edu (Subscribe)
This is the email list run by the MIT Assistive Technology Club. Subscribers can send and receive information about ideas, opportunities, and issues related to assistive technology.
